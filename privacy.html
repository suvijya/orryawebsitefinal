Privacy Policy

How Orrya collects, uses, and protects information.

Summary

We respect your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We aim to keep data collection minimal and use it to provide and improve our services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact data: name, email, message when you contact us.
  • Usage data: anonymised analytics such as pages visited and session durations.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser, device and operating system for security and performance.
  • Cookies: small files used to remember preferences and improve UX.

How We Use Data

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • To improve website functionality and content.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and security incidents.

Third Parties

We may use third-party services (analytics, hosting, email delivery). These providers have their own privacy terms; we select vendors that adhere to accepted security practices.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for session management and analytics. You can control cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may reduce website functionality.

Your Rights

You can request access to data we hold about you, request correction or deletion, or object to processing. To exercise these rights contact us at privacy@orrya.example.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data. No system is perfect; if a breach occurs we will notify affected users where required by law.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date appears at the top of this page. Please review periodically.

Contact

For privacy questions, email privacy@orrya.example or use the contact form on our Contact section.

Quick Facts

Controller: Orrya

Data retention: As required to provide services, typically 1 year unless stated otherwise.

Legal basis: Consent / Contract / Legitimate interest
